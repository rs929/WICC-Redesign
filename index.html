Our Mission

We aim to foster a supportive community of women and allies of all gender identities equipped with the resources needed to recognize and overcome challenges. By creating opportunities for technical and leadership growth, we work to ensure that people of all identities are able to discover and pursue their interests and talents in order to positively impact the future of tech.

WICC is dedicated towards fostering an inclusive space for individuals of all identities to feel empowered to speak on issues in the CIS community, both on- and off-campus. We offer community events, mentorship, and outreach opportunities. We're especially excited to foster a supportive network for everyone involved and extend our support outside of the Cornell community.
